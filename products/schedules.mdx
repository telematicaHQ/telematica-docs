---
title: "Schedules"
description: "Schedule commands or report generation for your customer vehicles"
---

Schedule conditional actions on EVs connected to your app using a simple API.
Schedules are only triggered when all the conditions are met, giving you granular control over when actions are performed.

![Schedules](/images/reference/schedules.svg "Schedules")

You can schedule the following commands:

- Start / Stop charging
- Activate / Deactivate HVAC systems
- Usage report (parking location, daily trips, charging history, odometer etc.)
