---
title: "Schedules"
description: "Schedule actions for EVs (Charging, HVAC controls)"
---

Schedule conditional actions on EVs connected to your app using a simple API.
Schedules are only triggered when all the conditions that you've specified are satisfied., giving you granular control over when the actions are performed.

![Schedules](/images/reference/schedules.svg "Schedules")

You can schedule the following commands:

- Start / Stop charging
- Activate / Deactivate HVAC systems
